SEDS•UPRM

Students for the Exploration and Development of Space, UPRM Chapter

Quick Info

Mission

To unify students from diverse academic backgrounds in the University of Puerto Rico - Mayagüez Campus, under the common interest for the development of the Space Industry in Puerto Rico.

Purpose

To encourage our members to develop their professional and academic skills by providing them the opportunity to do so via projects like:

  • Observation Nights
  • STEM Sessions with Renowned Puertorrican Scientists
  • Internships with the Arecibo Observatory
  • And more!
Vision

We aim to prepare undergraduate and graduate students of the University of Puerto Rico - Mayagüez Campus by helping them develop professional, academic and leadership skills in order to pioneer the Space Industry in Puerto Rico.

What exactly is SEDS?

The Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS) is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization that looks to inspire young people to participate and revolutionize the space industry. SEDS helps students develop their technical and leadership skills by providing opportunities to manage and participate in national projects as well as to attend conferences, publish their work, and develop their professional network, in order to help students become more effective in their present and future careers in industry, academia, government, and education.

How did SEDS start?

SEDS was founded as a chapter-based organization in 1980 at MIT by Peter Diamandis, at Princeton University by Scott Scharfman, and at Yale University by Richard Sorkin. SEDS-USA was founded as a national group in 1982 by Peter Diamandis, Bob Richards, and Todd Hawley. The largest student-run space organization in the world, it consists of an international community of high school, undergraduate, and graduate students from a diverse range of educational backgrounds in chapters all over the world, including Canada, India, Israel, Mexico, Nepal, Nigeria, Philippines, Spain, United Kingdom, and United States.

DPS

Data Processing System

DPS' main goal is to educate and involve the local community in space-related activities, like observation nights and interactive talks, as well as getting SEDS on the map.


FAO

Flight Activities Officers

FAO is mainly directed towards planning events for the members of the chapter. These include in-chapter movie nights, seminars, and even parties!

GNC

Guidance, Navigation, and Control System

GNC is the division that manages our projects. These include a rocketry team, making and maintaining high altitude balloons, and a CubeSat team.



FLIGHT

Directive Team

Focuses on maintaining SEDS•UPRM active and running, both within the university as an organization and as a chapter of SEDS•USA.
